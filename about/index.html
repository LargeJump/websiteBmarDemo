Britmar Power, a division of Britmar Marine Ltd., focuses on alternative energy markets, specializing in the storage and utilization of electrical energy. SuperB - Lithium Batteries

Low Maintenance

LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries are have a long lifespan and do not require regular maintenance unlike lead-acid batteries. A high resistance to thermal runaway, ensures stable performance while reducing the need for frequent monitoring.

High Density

High energy density in LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries means they can store more energy in a smaller and lighter package. This allows for greater power delivery in a compact form, making these batteries ideal for applications where space and weight are critical. High energy density also contributes to efficiency and performance, enhancing the functionality of devices they power.

Extended Life

LiFePO4 batteries have an extended life due to their chemical composition, resistance to thermal runaway, and low self-discharge rate. This stability leads to a higher number of charge-discharge cycles, ensuring reliable performance over time.

Fast Charging

Thermal stability and efficient chemical structure, allow for rapid energy intake without degrading the battery's lifespan or safety. Battery models come with integrated safety measures to ensure fast charging is safe through the use of a BMS.
