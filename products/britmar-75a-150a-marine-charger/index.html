Britmar 75A/150A Marine Charger

About The Product

Designed to operate in marine environments, the Britmar Marine line of battery chargers provides quality and durable functionalities that enable you to charge both 12V and 24V battery banks all within the same unit.

This product comes with an optional temperature sensor or power supply function to ensure safe operation and longevity.

Highlights

  • Selectable charge rate from 20%-100%
  • Charges wide selection of batteries
  • Lead Acid, Gel, AGM, and LiFePO4
  • Drip sheild included
  • LED fault indicator for troubleshooting
  • RS232 NEMA output
  • Optional temperature sensor
  • UL458 & CSA CERTIFIED
  • 2 year warranty
