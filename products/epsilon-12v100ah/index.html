    • About the product

    The Epsilon Lithium Iron Phosphate battery has the highest capacity in a standard casing available on the market. The highly sophisticated and integrated battery management system (BMS) brings safety, functionality, and ease of use together in a lightweight and robust casing. The fully automatic heating functionality, improved Bluetooth connectivity, and multi-connectable coated aluminum terminals make this battery a must-have.
