Andrena 12V10AH

  • Fast Charging
  • Lightweight
  • High Capacity
  • Low Maintenance

    • About the Product

    Lightweight, high-performance, safe and reliable lithium power battery, developed for motor sports. Designed in a standard rally casing. Suitable for 12V systems and for use in extreme environments. This battery has been designed to release large bursts of constant energy.
