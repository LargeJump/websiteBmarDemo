Kisae AC-DC Chargers

About The Product

KISAE’s smart battery chargers offer fully automatic, multi-stage charging and can charge three separate battery banks. These chargers utilize advanced algorithms to quickly and accurately recharge AGM, Gel, Flooded, or Lithium batteries, extending their lifespan. They can also charge different battery chemistries simultaneously.

The AC-DC chargers provide optimal in-vehicle or solar charging capabilities and maintain auxiliary batteries efficiently.

Features

Multi Stage Battery Charging

KISAE Battery Chargers use advanced charging algorithms to rapidly and safely replenish large capacity AGM, Gel, Flooded and Lithium batteries to peak efficiency. KISAE Chargers deliver four primary charge stages: Bulk, Absorption, Float and Maintenance. This results in a battery that is ready for use faster and improves battery life.

Battery Bank Prioritization

Chargers are Three-bank battery capable which allows the user to decide which battery is the most important. It will deliver a priority charge to Bank One, allowing this bank to get charged the quickest, then shift the cycle to battery banks Two and Three. In the event that all three banks need a recharge, an override function can recover all three banks quickly and evenly before switching back to Bank One Priority.

Off season Guard

By leaving your KISAE Charger connected to your batteries, even during off-season storage periods when the batteries are not in use, the Charger will automatically maintain your battery’s charge by running a fresh charge algorithm every 7 days. Charger’s intelligence feature ensures that the batteries are maintained so they are in top performance when the new season begins and you are ready to use your batteries.

Intelligent Charging

The smart KISAE Charger will regulate its output based on the loads connected to your battery banks. If one battery is discharging quickly due to loads on the battery, Kisae’s unique charging technology will automatically increase the rate of charge to that bank.
