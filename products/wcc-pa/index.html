WCC PA

About The Product

WallMax Plastic Frames are a family of lightweight mounting solutions made of glass-fibre reinforced plastic. Light Frames are specifically designed for applications where weight containment is essential, for instance where the wall supporting the installation is light in construction and has low bearing capacity.

Each lightweight WallMax frame has different characteristics, whose combination offers a wide variety of uses and applications, especially inside cabinets, data centres, and for the automation sector.

Material

PA 6.6 30% Glass Filled
