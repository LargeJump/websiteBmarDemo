    About the Product

    Lightweight, high-performance, safe and reliable lithium power battery, developed for motor ports. Designed in a standard rally casing. Suitable for 12V systems and for use in extreme environments. This battery has been designed to release large bursts of constant energy.

    Open Circuit Voltage 13.2V
    Self Discharge <3% per month
